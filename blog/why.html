H ello! I’m Liam, and this is my website. What is the purpose of this website? Well, I’m glad you asked. In short, I’m making this website as a place to put all of my stuff. What stuff? Again, thank you for asking. I’ll be filling this website with lists of my favorite things, different ideas I’m obsessively sketching, pictures I take, and really anything else I can think of.

I kind of want this to be where post stuff instead of my social media pages. Social media platforms seem to be going to shit recently, and I also don’t tend to like how they make me feel when I’m on them for extended periods of time. Here, I can make what I want without worrying about likes or ads or the fact that my content exists on a platform that is liable to crumble under the weight of the maniacal CEO in charge.

So grab your favorite RSS reader and come along while I figure this out :)
