  • Visitor Center for Guthrie, Oklahoma

As a movie set, Guthrie, Oklahoma has starred in titles like Twister, Rain Man, Stillwater, and many others. Ironically, Guthrie doesn’t have a movie theater of its own (at time of design). So in addition to being a visitor center for Oklahoma’s historica capital, this proposal also functions as a drive-in movie theather.
